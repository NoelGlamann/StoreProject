*ATTN* COVID-19 Updates


Because of the pandemic, we have had to make some changes in our store. We are sorry for the inconvenience and appreciate your willingness to cooperate.

  • Orders will have to be placed from the classroom through our website or our order forms.
  • Our staff will deliver your orders at your designated break time.
  • All employees will be gloved and make minimal contact.
  • Our store will remain closed on Fridays, but the snack table will no longer be available.
  • We will also be taking sanitizing processes very seriously to keep you safe.

We miss seeing your smiling faces, but we can't wait to see them again!
